About me

Klavdiya Yessenzhulova
  • Member of the Kazakhstan Bar Association Chamber of legal consultants;
  • Arbitrator of the International Arbitration “IUS”;
  • Expert of the MOST Business Incubator;
  • Author of the course for the entrepreneurs “Lawyer for yourself”.
Education
  • 2010 – Caspian Social University (Adilet High Law School);
  • 2013 – Arbitration Academy. Paris.
Experience

More than 10 years of legal experience. She worked as a lawyer in the transportation company, gas wholesale trade company, holding that was included in the 500 largest companies in Kazakhstan, international law firm. Klavdiya also:

  • accompanied M&A deals;
  • gave opinions on wide range of matters;
  • repaired document flow in different companies;
  • formulated typical contracts;
  • prepared labor contracts and also managed paperwork on the main activity of the dental clinic, shipping company and translation company;
  • registered intellectual property rights for the translation company;
  • registered rights on real estate;
  • represented interests of clients in different courts;
  • participated in cases as an arbitrator;
  • made consultations on wide range of matters.
Languages

Russian, English, Kazakh (conversational), French (conversational).
